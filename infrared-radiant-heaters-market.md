<p><p>The infrared radiant heaters market can be categorized into high-intensity and low-intensity types. High-intensity infrared heaters emit concentrated heat, making them suitable for large spaces or industrial applications, delivering immediate warmth. They are often used in outdoor settings and warehouses. In contrast, low-intensity infrared heaters provide a gentler, more uniform heat distribution, ideal for residential and commercial spaces. Their slower heat-up time makes them suitable for areas requiring sustained warmth, such as bathrooms or small rooms.</p></p>
