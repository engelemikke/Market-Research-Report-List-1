<p><p>The semiconductor manufacturing equipment market is divided into two main types: front-end equipment and back-end equipment. Front-end equipment involves processes that create integrated circuits on silicon wafers, including lithography, etching, and deposition tools essential for chip fabrication. Back-end equipment, on the other hand, focuses on the packaging and assembly of finished chips, including testing and characterization tools to ensure functionality and reliability. Both segments play crucial roles in the semiconductor supply chain, enabling efficient production and enhancing device performance.</p></p>
