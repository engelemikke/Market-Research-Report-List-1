<p><p>The SWIR camera market is primarily divided into two types: SWIR area scan cameras and SWIR line scan cameras. SWIR area scan cameras capture images in a two-dimensional format, making them suitable for applications such as inspection and surveillance. In contrast, SWIR line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, allowing for high-speed scanning of moving objects, ideal for industrial automation and quality control. Both types utilize shortwave infrared technology for enhanced imaging capabilities across various industries.</p></p>
